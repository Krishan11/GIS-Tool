Archelaus

Archelaus was a leading military general of the of Pontus. He was the greatest general that had served under King Mithridates VI.He was Mithridates Favourite General In his early life, Archelaus was a Greek nobleman. His ancestors came from those Greeks who arrived in Anatolia after the expedition of King Alexander the Great .His brother Neoptolemus and his family were active in the Pontic Court with Neoptolemus they both rose to prominence during the wars of Mithridates VI on the northern shores of the Black Sea.Archelaus was a important Pontic general during the First Mithridatic War 89 BC-85 BC. In 88 BC as a part of the First Mithridatic War.

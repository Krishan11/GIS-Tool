Cleopatra

Cleopatra was born in 69 BC -68 BC. When her father died,she became co-regent with her brother Ptolemy XIII.They were married as per Egyptian custom but later she ruled solely.She was beautiful and was considered to be a highly intelligent woman and an astute politician. She brought wealth and prosperity to the country that was at one time bankrupt and was split by civil war.In 48 BC,conflict in Rome between Julius Caesar and Pompey started.Pompey was murdered on the orders of Ptolemy ,Caesar followed and he and Cleopatra became lovers,and she became queen once again with Roman military support after being exiled by her brother.

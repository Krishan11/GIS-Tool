Gaius Cassius Longinus

Gaius Cassius Longinus was before 85 BC was involved in the assasination to kill Julius Caesar.He was also a Roman senator and the brother in-law of Marcus Junius Brutus.He married Junia Tertia,who was the daughter of Servilia Caepionis and had one son with her .He also took part in Battle of Carrhae and lost by Marcus Licinius Crassus against the Parthians.In 44 BC Cassius became praetor peregrinus and was promised the governorship of Syria for the following year .Cassius was man of considerably ability and a good soldier but in politics he was actuated by vanity and ambition and had uncontollable temper.

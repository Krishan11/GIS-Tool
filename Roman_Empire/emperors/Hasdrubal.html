Hasdrubal

Hasdrubal the Boeotarch was the leading military leader in the Third Punic War which was the third and last war between the Carthagians and the Roman Republic and which resulted in ultimate downfall of a once wealthy and prosperous nation,Carthage.He was a great leader and Commander of Carthage as he gave hard time to Romans in order to suppress Carthage .He trained his army and equippped them with all the neccessary equipments and weapons to defend themselves against the Romans.But due to the treaty signed with the Romans after the Second Punic War,Carthagain army was weakened as compared to the Romans and thus they lost the battle with Romans and Hasdrubal then surrundered himself to the Romans.

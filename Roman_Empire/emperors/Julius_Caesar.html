Julius Caesar

Gaius Julius Caesar was born in 100 BC was a Roman general, statesman,and a Consul. His Quote "Omnia gallia in tres partes divisa est" meaning all Gaul is divided into three parts" was very famous, which was used in many books. At the age of 16 Caesar was the head of family as his father died in 85 BC. He played an important role in the demise of the Roman Republic and the rise of the Roman Empire.He was also elected military tribune in 70 B.C. Caesar was elected quaestor and obtained a seat in the Senate, he married granddaughter of Sulla,named Pompeia. Caesar supported Gnaeus Pompey and helped him get an extraordinary generalship against the Mediterranean pirates, later extended to command of the war against King Mithridates in Asia Minor.Much of Caesars life is known from his own accounts of his military campaigns and from other contemporary sources.

Lucius Cornelius Sulla Felix

Sulla was born in poor patrician family 138 B.C.,he gained wealth from a woman named Nicopolis and his stepmother, which allowed him to enter the political ring . During the Jugurthine War,the Arpinum-born, novus homo Marius selected Sulla for his quaestor. Although this choice led to many political conflict, but it was wise militarily. Sulla in his leadership ,resolved the war by persuading a neighboring African king to kidnap Jugurtha for the Romans. In 88 B.C Sulla went to Greece in charge of the war against Mithradates. By the spring of 87 most of Greece was in his power, and after a long siege he captured Athens in 86.

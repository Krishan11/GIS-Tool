Marcus Agrippa

Marcus Vipsanius Agrippa was born in 63 B.C. was a powerful deputy,son-in-law and a close friend of Augustus as both of them were educated together,(the first Roman emperor).He was the man responsible for the victory at the Battle of Actium in 31 B.C (faught with Mark Antony), and during Augustus reign he faught with various rebellions, founded various colonies, and administered several places of the Roman Empire. He was not liked by the Roman Aristocracy and was not considered as a Modest Person.In his own interest he maintained a inferior role in relation to Augustus,but other than that he never felt himself inferior to no one else.

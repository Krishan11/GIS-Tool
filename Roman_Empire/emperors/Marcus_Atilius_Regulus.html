Marcus Atilius Regulus

Marcus Atilius Regulus, was born in 3 century B.C. was a Roman general and statesman whose career was polished by legend.he was seen by the Romans as a model of heroic endurance. He was one of the commanders in the Roman naval expedition that shattered the Carthaginian fleet at Cape Ecnomus.The invaders were so successful that the other consul, Lucius Manlius Vulso Longus, was recalled to Rome, leaving Regulus behind to finish the war.He served as consul in 267 and 256.

Marcus Junius Brutus

Marcus Junius Brutus ,also called as Quintus Caepio Brutus was born in 85 BC near Philippi,Macedonian.He was a Roman Politician and led an important role in conspiracy that killed Julius Caesar.His Father was killed by Pompey the Great in 77 BC.His mother was half sister of Cato the Younger .He held his uncle in high regard as his uncle adopted him when nobody knew him .His political career started when he became an assistant to Cato,during his governorship of Cyprus.Later,He became a rich man and got married to Claudia Pulchra.He espoused as a Stoic and was admired by Cicero and other Roman Aristocrats and after his death he became a symbol of resistance to tyranny.

Pompey

Gnaeus Pompeius Magnus was born on 29 September 106 BC . He was also known as Pompey the Great and was a military commander and a great political leader of Roman Republic.He came from a wealthy and prosperous Italian family.His success as a young general of Roman Republic gave him the confidence to advance directly to became the consul ,without meeting the normal ofice requirements.He was consul three times and celebrated three triumphs.He was married to Julius Caesar daughter Julia ,but after the death of Julia Pompey and Caesar contended for the leadership of the Roman Senate ,leading to a civil war,and was defeated by Julius Caesar.Pompey died on September of 48 BC at the age of 58 in Pelusium,Ptolemaic Egypt.

Publius Quinctilius Varus

Publius Qunictilius Varus ,born in 46 BC in Cremona ,Roman Republic was a Roman Politician and general under Emperor Augustus.He is famous for his defeat at the Battle of Teutoburg Forest where he lost his 3 legions and his life when attacked by Arminius who was German tribal leader.He came of an old patrician family ,with no political influence.His father was one of the assassins of Julius Caesar and committed suicide after the Battle of Philippi.Varus married the daughter of Marcus Agrippa.After Agrippa died ,it was Varus who delivered the funeral eulogy,Thus his political career was boosted and he became a consul with Tiberius,Augustus stepson and future emperor.

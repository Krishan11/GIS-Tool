Scipio Aemilianus

Publius Cornelius Scipio Aemilianus Africanus Numantinus also known as Scipio Aemilianus was born in 185 BC and was a leading general and a great politician of Roman Republic.He commanded Roman Republic at the final siege and desturction of Carthage in 146 BC.Scipio was the second son of Lucius Aemilius Paullus Macedonius who was the hero of the Third Macedonian War .Soon after Scipio birth Paullus divorced his wife Papiria ,thus he was adopted by Publius Cornelius Scipio and his name was changed to Publius Cornelius Scipio Aemilianus .He died in Rome,Italy in 129 BC but his name will forever be associated with the destruction of Carthage as the horror he expressed at its fate speaks to his humanity .He was considered to be a man of culture and refinement.

Battle of Athens and Piraeus

The Siege of Athens and Piraeus was a first Mithridatic war that took place in Athens from 87 BC to 86 BC ,was a bitterly faught clash that only ended when the defendars of the city pulled out by the sea after the fall of the city of Athens. The battle took place between the forces Roman Republic led by Lucius Cornelius Sulla Felix and the fleet of Kingdom of Pontus and the Greco Pontus Forces which were led by Archelaus.During the battle ,large parts of southern Greece rose against the ROmans ,and for a short period it looked as if Romans authority would collapse in Greece but in the end the War Resulted in Roman Victory with very few Casualties.

Battle of Dyrrhachium

The Battle of Dyrrachium took place on 10 July 10 BC at Dyrrhachium was a battle faught between Julius Caesar with a total strength of 45000+ and the army led by Gnaeus Pompey with the strength of 15000 and the support of Roman Senate.In the battle pompey held an attack of six legions on Caesar's line where it joined the sea,Heavily outnumbering the Caesarian troops ,the pompeian army broke through the weakened fortification causing Caesar's force to pull back from onslaught.At first Caesar personally tried to stem the war ,but then later he realised the danger to his army and backed off.The Battle was indecisive but is regarded as a victory for pompey.

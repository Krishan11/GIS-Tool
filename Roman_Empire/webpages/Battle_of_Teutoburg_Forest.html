Battle of Teutoburg Forest

The Battle of Teutoburg Forest took place in 9 CE(Common Era) between Germanic tribes led by Arminius of the Cherusci and the Romans led by Publius Quinctilius Varus.Varus forces included three legions,six cohorts of auxilliary troops and three squandrons of cavalry but the Germanic warriors attacked the Romans with light swords ,large lances and narrow blended short spears and surrounded the entire Roman army and rained down javelins on them .Arminius ,who grew up in Rome and knew Romans tactics and directed his troops to counter them effectively.The Romans wanted to escape ,but marched into another trap which Arminius had set for them at the foot of Kalkriese Hill.The Battle ended in Roman defeat as Arminius conquered all Roman forts ,garrision and cities.Upon Hearing the defeat ,the Emperor Augustus was so shaken that he stood butting his head against the walls and shouting "Quintili Vare, legiones redde!â€œ ('Quintilius Varus, give me back my legions!')
